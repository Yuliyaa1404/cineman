    Common questions

  • How soon after the broadcast does the movies appear?

    The movies usually appear simultaneously with the premiere in the USA, taking into account the time difference. The difference can be several hours – this time is needed for translation and subtitles overlay.

  • What is a rental?

    Rental is a time–limited access to a certain series. A one-time purchase of one series for rent is limited to a period of up to 60 days.

  • Is it possible to buy the movies forever?

    Some movies are available for purchase with unlimited access. In the service, they are marked with the text "The series is available for purchase forever and is not included in the main subscription".

    Subscribe and payment

  • How do I subscribe?

    To subscribe, register in the service or log in to an existing account, go to the "Purchases" section and select the subscription you are interested in.

  • How do I buy a subscription to the service?

    You can buy a subscription: using the mobile version of the website in a browser on a mobile device or in the Amediateka app on iOS, Android, Android TV, Apple TV and Smart TV.

  • How do I find out what is included in the subscription?

    The main subscription includes all series and films of the service, with the exception of several projects that are sold separately.
